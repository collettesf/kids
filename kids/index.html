Chores

A child now is responsible enough to do chores and can get rewarded

  • Sweeping is a good chore

  • Helping with dishes

  • Picking up their room

Schedule

A schedule is essential for all kids to keep on task

  • Set out daily tasks your child must do.

Discipline

Your child should know wrong from right at this age

  • Try to talk it over with your child before punishment.

  • If your child still will not listen give them time out.

  • If your child still will not listen, give them a light spanking.
